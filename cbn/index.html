Overview

This page lists the chapter summaries for the The Consumer Credit sourcebook (CONC) and provides direct download links to both Markdown (.md) and Word (.docx) versions.

The reason why I posted these files is that the entire CONC is well over 400 pages. Therefore hard to turn into a single CBN chapter. By breaking the CONC up in chapters, the work can be prioritized. The relevance assessment and the relevance report show the relevance of each chapter.

Note, Chapter 7 was too big to summarize in one go, so it is split into two.
